# For Anyone

You don't need a role. You don't need permission. You don't need
expertise. You don't need followers.

The smallest possible action this manifesto invites:

**Read it with one other person.**

That's it.

If you do that — pick someone who you can have an honest conversation
with, send them the manifesto, wait a week, then talk for one hour
about what landed.

You will notice two things during that hour:

1. The conversation itself will be different from your usual ones.
   The manifesto's vocabulary creates new things to discuss.

2. Something will shift between you and that person. Not always
   big — sometimes very small. But something.

That shift is the smallest possible LifeBite of this project.

## If You Want to Go Further

Form a "Context Family" cell. The instructions are in
`manifesto/en.md` (Part 6.3) or `manifesto/ko.md` (Part 6.4).

Three to five people, one year, simple operating principles. Try.

## If Something Happens

Open an issue here labeled `cell-report` and share what you learn.
Your honest report — including failure — becomes the seed for the
next cell.

That is how the chain propagates.

That is how the manifesto becomes more than a manifesto.

— Welcome.
