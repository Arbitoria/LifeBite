# For the Executor

This folder is for people who could *act on* the manifesto — those with
the position, tools, capital, or community to start small experiments
in any of the directions the manifesto sketches.

## Common Threads

Across all roles, three principles apply:

1. **Start small.** The manifesto explicitly rejects grand blueprints.
   One small tool, one classroom, one cell of five people — that is
   the unit of legitimate action.

2. **Be honest.** This work has no certainty of impact. Anyone acting
   on it should accept that they may not see the outcome of their action.
   That uncertainty is part of the design.

3. **Document.** Whatever you try, write it down. Open an issue here
   with the `cell-report` label. Your record becomes the seed for
   the next attempt.

## A Note on Time

If you find yourself wanting to act on this manifesto, please don't
rush. The work that lasts longest usually started slowly.

Read fully. Sit with it for a week. Then act small.
