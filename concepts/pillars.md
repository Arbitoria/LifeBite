# The Three Pillars

The manifesto's three structural pillars are detailed in the full
text at [`../manifesto/en.md`](../manifesto/en.md) (Part 3).

| Pillar | Now | New System |
|--------|-----|------------|
| **Education** | Individual evaluation, surface signals | Contextual learning, inner cultivation, gap training |
| **Network** | Exposure algorithm, ad-funded attention | Trust algorithm, prepayment model |
| **Identity** | Default exposure, manual blocking | Default block, ticket-based permission |

All three are unified by one principle: **the Sovereign Filter** —
*I decide what enters my consciousness.*

See full discussion in [`../manifesto/en.md`](../manifesto/en.md) Part 3
and structured analysis in [`glossary.md`](glossary.md) Section 3.
