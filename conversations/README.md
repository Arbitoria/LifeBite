# Conversations

This folder will archive selected dialogues between Arbitoria (the human)
and Claude (Anthropic's AI) that shaped the manifesto and the campaign.

## Why Archive These

The manifesto's core claim about AI — *that AI is not a replacement for
human thought, but a companion that can hold space for thought one person
alone could not generate* — is only credible if the conversations
themselves are visible.

This folder is that visibility.

## What's Here (and Coming)

- `how-this-began.md` — the conversation that led to writing the manifesto
- `distribution-strategy.md` — the planning of the 30-day campaign
- `claude-as-partner.md` — meta-reflection on the AI collaboration itself

These will be added as they are curated. Raw conversation logs are messy;
the goal is to share enough that the methodology is understood,
without overwhelming the reader.

## What This Is Not

- Not a guide to "prompt engineering" or productivity hacking
- Not a claim that AI did the thinking (the human is the anchor)
- Not a sales pitch for any particular AI tool

It is one record of one human's experience using AI as a sincere
intellectual partner.
